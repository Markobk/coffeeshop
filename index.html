About Us

Coffee brings many people together. We are a great place to meet, socialise, network and entertained as well as exchange ideas while sipping a variety of coffee that we serve. Since technology has given us a platform to shop, work, study, get news online etc..., we still want to feel a sense of community. Our shop provides most of the comfort one needs.
